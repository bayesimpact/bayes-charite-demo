01 — The challenge

Medical teams deserve to own their AI

Today

Locked into vendor solutions

  • ✕Costly, opaque off-the-shelf tools built by external engineers
  • ✕Disconnected from real clinical protocols and local knowledge
  • ✕Data leaves the institution — privacy and sovereignty risks
  • ✕Slow to update when guidelines change
  • ✕No evaluation framework — no way to measure quality or trust
  • ✕Every new use case requires a new contract, a new vendor
With the Bayes Platform

Medical teams become the authors

  • ✓Clinicians configure agents in plain language — no code required
  • ✓Knowledge base built from your own protocols, guidelines, FAQs
  • ✓Hosted on EU servers, HDS-certified — data stays in the institution
  • ✓Update the document base and the agent updates instantly
  • ✓Built-in evaluation framework with scoring and iteration tracking
  • ✓Every agent becomes a reusable digital common for other hospitals

04 — Technical architecture

Built for healthcare. Designed for trust.

🔒

Sovereign & open-source

Hosted on EU servers, HDS-certified. No data sent to third-party AI providers for training. Full code transparency.

🔍

RAG architecture

Retrieval-Augmented Generation — the agent answers from your institution's document base, not generic internet knowledge.

🤖

Multi-agent support

Conversational RAG agents, structured data extraction, questionnaire agents, eligibility agents — all on one platform.

🖱️

No-code configuration

System prompt in plain language. Document import via drag-and-drop. No technical skills required from clinical teams.

📊

Built-in eval framework

Golden dataset testing, scoring by dimension (accuracy, completeness, format, tone), full iteration history.

🌐

Open-source publishing

Documentation templates, licence management, structured reuse pipeline — every agent can become a digital common.

🛡️

GDPR & AI Act compliant

Low-risk classification. No personal data in standard scope. Immediate session data deletion. Full audit trail.

⚡

Rapid deployment

Functional prototype in under 6 weeks. Designed for iterative improvement without engineering dependencies.

08 — Project organisation · Charité × Bayes Impact

How we work together

💚
Budget — fully pro bono

Bayes Impact is a nonprofit funded through philanthropic partners. We do not charge for our support or platform use. Pro bono deployment support within Charité's infrastructure is available if needed.

🔓 Open-source — free forever, no lock-in

Both the platform and the AI solution will be made freely available to Charité. The free access is permanent — no dependency on Bayes Impact. Charité can install the platform on its own servers at any time and operate it fully independently.

👥
Team

Multidisciplinary team from Bayes Impact — AI engineers, developers, project and methodological leads. Primarily France-based, available for in-person sessions in Berlin whenever needed.

📅
Calendar

Start: Summer 2026
Pilot: 2–3 months

Mid-point check-in — early September
Final joint review — early October
📝
Legal framework

A dedicated partnership agreement may be drafted. The existing Charité × AP-HP framework established in France can serve as a basis for structuring the German collaboration.

🧭
Governance

Joint Charité × Bayes Impact steering committee — intentionally lean to maintain agility. Members to be defined jointly.
